Why Vice President?

Every role in our organization plays a vital part, and I have deep respect for all of them. After years of service on the CTS Board, however, I know exactly where my strengths create the most impact.

My commitment to the Illinois Tamil Community goes back to 2006, when I served on the Board of Directors for the Central Illinois Tamil Sangam and taught at the Champaign Tamil School for seven years. That experience shaped how I think about service: it is not about recognition, but about showing up for my community year after year.

I have carried that same commitment into my work on the CTS Board, managing budgets and introducing financial transparency practices that our organization still uses today. Yet, what truly drives me is not the paperwork or the ledger. It is the people.

My greatest strength lies in the human element — motivating volunteers, building relationships within our community, and uniting committed individuals into a coordinated team that delivers results. That is the exact energy and focus I want to dedicate to our community as your Vice President.
